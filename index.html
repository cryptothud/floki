HOW TO BUY

1

Create a Wallet

Floki Inu is available on the Ethereum Mainnet. MetaMask & Trust Wallet are the market leaders in ETH wallets. Visit www.metamask.io or www.trustwallet.com to download the extensions and set up a wlalet. On mobile? Get MetaMask or TrustWallet's app for iPhone or Android.

2

Fund Your Wallet

Buy ETH through MetaMask/TrustWallet or transfer it to your MetaMask/TrustWallet address from another wallet (e.g. Binance).

3

Visit Uniswap

On the home page of www.floki-inu.com click on the "Buy on Uniswap" button. This will open a Dapp Exchange called "Uniswap." A popup will appear, click the "import" button, which will load the Floki token into Uniswap.

4

Swap $ETH for $FLOKI

After clicking "Import," click "Connect Wallet" and follow the steps with your selected wallet. Finally, enter the total amount of ETH you would like to swap, set the slippage to 12%, and click "Swap." Confirm swap, and HODL to the moon!!!!
